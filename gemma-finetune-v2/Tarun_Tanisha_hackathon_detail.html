FeatureURLWhat it does
PathoHunt 3D /game/pathohunt-3d Battle game: pick a pathogen boss (COVID MPro, EGFR, HIV Protease, BACE1…), AI generates drug molecules as "ammunition", MAMMAL scores binding affinity as hit/miss. 8 boss levels, 4 difficulty tiers, XP + badge rewards.
3D Experiment /run-3d 3D protein structure viewer + live AI molecule generation. Enter a target → RCSB PDB loads the protein → Gemma4 generates drug candidates with rationale → scores displayed in real time.

Tutorial System /tutorial In-game tutorial guide teaching drug discovery concepts (binding affinity, SMILES, Lipinski Ro5) to students in game language.
Social Feed /feed Share experiments, see other researchers' discoveries. Collaborative science platform.
Ask AI /ask-ai Chat interface for students to ask drug discovery questions in plain English.
Mission Board /missions Gamified challenges: "Defeat COVID boss", "Generate 5 drug-like molecules", "Score above 80% binding affinity".
PathoHunt 3D Game Levels: Influenza Neuraminidase · SARS-CoV-2 MPro · HIV Protease · EGFR Kinase · BACE1 · DPP-4 · CDK2 · Cancer (generic). Each has a patient backstory, resistance mutation pool, and escalating difficulty tiers (Junior → Nobel Prize level).
