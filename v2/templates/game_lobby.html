How PathoHunt Works

🧬
1. Pick a Pathogen Boss

Each boss is a real protein involved in disease. Higher levels = tougher targets.

⚗️
2. Design Your Molecule

Use AI (Gemma4) to generate drug candidates, or enter your own SMILES.

⚔️
3. Launch an Attack

Your molecules are scored using real cheminformatics. Better scores = more boss damage.

🏆
4. Win the Battle

Defeat the boss by designing a molecule that scores above the win threshold. Earn XP and badges.

Science disclaimer: All scores are computational simulations only. A high score does not mean the molecule is a real drug candidate. PathoHunt is an educational tool for learning drug discovery concepts.
